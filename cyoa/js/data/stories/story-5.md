# The Sunken Library

## Chapter 1
### Title
The Flooded Archive

### Content
During a storm surge, a hidden stone doorway opens beneath the harbor lighthouse. You descend with a lantern and discover an impossible library submerged to the waist in seawater. Shelves stretch into darkness, and every book spine glows with faint blue ink.

### Choices
1. Climb onto a dry reading platform and inspect the nearest shelf -> 2
2. Follow a trail of glowing pages drifting deeper into the stacks -> 3

## Chapter 2
### Title
The Atlas of Tides

### Content
You pull a heavy atlas from the shelf and seawater drains from its pages without wetting your hands. Maps redraw themselves while you watch, revealing hidden currents and forgotten islands. A margin note appears in fresh ink: "The Indexer is awake."

### Choices
1. Call out to the Indexer and ask for help -> 4
2. Use the atlas to locate the restricted vault -> 5

## Chapter 3
### Title
Whispering Aisles

### Content
As you wade between shelves, whispers echo from titles in dozens of languages. Some books open themselves and display your memories as if they were stories already written. At the end of the aisle, a bronze elevator cage waits, its door open and gears turning slowly.

### Choices
1. Step into the elevator and descend -> 5
2. Stay in the aisles and follow the loudest whisper -> 4

## Chapter 4
### Title
The Last Indexer

### Content
An old caretaker in a tide-stained coat appears with a lantern made of glass coral. He is the Last Indexer, guardian of the catalog. He explains the library is sinking because one forbidden volume was moved from its proper shelf, breaking the balance of the whole archive.

### Choices
1. Ask him to lead you to the misplaced volume -> 6
2. Refuse and search for the volume on your own -> 5

## Chapter 5
### Title
The Restricted Vault

### Content
You enter a circular vault where chains of silver script hang from the ceiling. At the center floats a black-bound book titled The Hollow Current. When you touch it, the water level surges and the library trembles. A choice forms in the air like engraved fog: restore order, or claim forbidden knowledge.

### Choices
The End


## Chapter 6
### Title
Dawn Above the Harbor

### Content
The misplaced volume slides home with a soft click, and the water begins to recede. Bells ring from the lighthouse above as morning breaks. The Indexer bows once and hands you a waterproof library card stamped with your name and a new title: Apprentice Keeper.

### Choices
The End

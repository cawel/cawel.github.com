# Echoes of the Perseid Gate

## Keywords
- spaceship
- anomaly
- gate

## Chapter 1
### Title
Arrival at Perseid

### Content
You command the research vessel Asterion on a high-risk mission: assess the Perseid Gate before the flare window closes. Outside the bridge glass, the ring hangs like a broken star and hums in your teeth. It is older than any human record, and its unstable harmonics now ripple near inhabited routes. Command’s order is clear—determine whether it can be stabilized or must be destroyed before civilian traffic wakes.

### Choices
1. Begin close-range harmonic mapping immediately -> 2
2. Hold distance and run passive analysis first -> 3

## Chapter 2
### Title
Shear Zone

### Content
At close range, your ship phase-skips in microbursts and appears in two positions for split seconds. Navigation officer Rhea warns that repeated shear will tear your hull if you linger. But the data stream is incredible: enough to identify a potential transit corridor and manual control pylons.

### Choices
1. Send an unmanned probe through the corridor -> 4
2. Back off and integrate with passive analysis -> 3

## Chapter 3
### Title
Lock Rhythm

### Content
From safe distance, astrophysicist Kenji decodes an eleven-minute pulse pattern tied to local magnetar wind. The gate is only safe during narrow lock intervals; outside them, transit fragments matter. You have one viable window in thirty-seven minutes.

### Choices
1. Use the window for a crewed transit to inspect from inside -> 5
2. Skip transit and transmit closure recommendation to command -> 9

## Chapter 4
### Title
Probe Debrief

### Content
The probe returns coated in unknown crystal dust and carrying a maintenance warning: "Lattice breach progressing. Calibrate or collapse." Star maps in its memory show linked systems not on current charts. The gate is failing machinery, not random anomaly.

### Choices
1. Attempt live calibration at the control pylons -> 6
2. Prepare controlled demolition charges for full closure -> 7

## Chapter 5
### Title
Dead System Beacon

### Content
You transit through a safe lock and emerge in a derelict linked system. A surviving maintenance AI requests emergency lattice repair and confirms cascade risk to both systems if no action is taken. It provides calibration geometry and warns that closure is possible but irreversible.

### Choices
1. Trust the AI guidance and attempt calibration -> 6
2. Return immediately and execute closure protocol -> 7

## Chapter 6
### Title
Three Overrides

### Content
Calibration requires three synchronized manual overrides on exposed pylons while radiation spikes pulse through the scaffold. You can distribute risk across the whole team for maximum completion odds, or assign one runner to finish fastest at near-certain fatal cost.

### Choices
1. Execute full-team synchronized override -> 10
2. Take the runner baton yourself to finish in time -> 8

## Chapter 7
### Title
Closure Clock

### Content
Charges are armed and aligned. A clean detonation will collapse the gate into inert fragments and end cascade risk, but it will also sever all linked routes forever. A mistimed blast could worsen instability. You have one final confirmation before the timer crosses no-return.

### Choices
1. Confirm detonation and seal the horizon -> 9
2. Abort closure and return to calibration plan -> 6

## Chapter 8
### Title
One Runner

### Content
You reject sacrificing your crew and take the runner baton yourself. You complete the final override as a radiation bloom consumes the pylon bridge. The lattice stabilizes and cascade risk disappears, but your suit telemetry flatlines before recovery drones can reach you. The gate survives for future generations because you paid in full.

### Choices
The End

## Chapter 9
### Title
Sealed Horizon

### Content
You confirm detonation. The ring fractures into silver debris and local space settles. Command praises the decision: safe, decisive, irreversible. Humanity loses immediate access to unknown linked systems, but billions remain protected from cascade failure. You kept your crew alive. You also closed a door history may never reopen.

### Choices
The End

## Chapter 10
### Title
Gatekeepers of Dawn

### Content
Your team executes all three overrides in sequence under lethal conditions and survives with injuries but complete success. The lattice rethreads into stable geometry. The maintenance AI certifies negligible cascade risk. Command establishes a multinational oversight charter using your calibration protocol as law. Years later, cadets still study your mission as proof that exploration and responsibility must travel together.

### Choices
The End
